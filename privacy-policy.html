Privacy Policy for Dry Days

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Summary: Dry Days does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Introduction

Welcome to Dry Days. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage for the Dry Days mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information.

Dry Days is designed with your privacy in mind. The App:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect usage data
  • Does not track your activity
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data

Data Storage

Any data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

Dry Days does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, Dry Days is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
